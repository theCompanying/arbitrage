**Purpose:** Automatically find and score new product opportunities.

**Features:**
- [ ] Scheduled scraping of AliExpress categories
- [ ] Auto-import products matching criteria (margin > 25%, BSR < 50K, reviews < 500)
- [ ] Background job queue for API calls
- [ ] Duplicate detection (prevent re-importing same products)
- [ ] Daily digest email of top new opportunities

**Files:**
- `apps/web/src/lib/product-discovery.ts`
- `apps/web/src/app/api/discovery/scan/route.ts`
- `packages/database/prisma/schema.prisma` (add DiscoveryRule model)

**Priority:** HIGH - Reduces manual research time
