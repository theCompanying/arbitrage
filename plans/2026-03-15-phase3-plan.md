## Phase 3 Goal

Build product research dashboard UI and identify first 3-5 viable products for launch.

## Priority Tasks

### 1. Product Research Dashboard (Week 1)

**Dashboard Features**
- Product candidates list with filters (margin %, BSR, demand score)
- Side-by-side AliExpress vs Amazon comparison
- One-click import from AliExpress URL
- Status workflow: Research → Evaluating → Approved → Launched

**Product Detail View**
- Fee breakdown visualization
- Profitability scoring (0-100)
- GO/MAYBE/NO_GO recommendations
- Supplier info and contact

### 2. Manual Product Research (Parallel)

While building dashboard:
- Identify 10-15 candidate products manually
- Use Helium 10 / Jungle Scout free trials
- Order samples from top 3-5 suppliers
- Evaluate quality, packaging, shipping time

### 3. Apply for API Access (Critical Path)

**Amazon PA-API**
- URL: https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/associates/api
- Requires: Amazon Associates account, website URL, traffic description
- Timeline: 1-3 business days for approval

**AliExpress API**
- URL: https://partners.aliexpress.com/
- Requires: AliExpress account, application description
- Timeline: 1-2 business days

## Economics Targets

**Product Criteria**
- Minimum 25% net margin after all fees
- Amazon selling price: $20-50 (sweet spot for impulse buys)
- BSR < 50,000 in main category (proven demand)
- < 500 reviews on top listings (beatable competition)
- Lightweight (< 2 lbs) to minimize FBA fees

**Launch Timeline**
- Week 1-2: Dashboard + manual research
- Week 3-4: Sample evaluation + Seller Central setup
- Week 5-6: First order placement (300-500 units)
- Week 7-10: Shipping + Amazon listing creation
- Week 11-12: Launch + PPC campaigns

**Capital Requirements**
- Samples: $200-500
- First inventory order: $3,000-5,000
- Shipping + customs: $1,000-2,000
- Amazon PPC (Month 1): $500-1,000
- **Total: $5,000-8,500**
